Giti Manufacturing

Giti Tire utilizes a global and green process to endure quality manufacturing for all types of drivers.

Giti Tire’s recently opened manufacturing center in the United States adds to existing centers in China and Indonesia, enabling a truly global presence. In total, combined production capacity exceeds 100 million tires per year, serving markets throughout the world.

Quality and Consistent Manufacturing is at the heart of what Giti does. Our distributors and drivers rely on the best products, which require extensive research, manufacturing equipment, materials, human resources, and quality control to meet the high demands of today’s consumers.

China

With six production centers in four locations strategically placed in China, Giti has the technology to produce all types of tires to meet needs of customers for both the Chinese and global markets. Each of the facilities has full production and environmental certifications, and consideration is always given to making a positive impact back to the local communities, by utilizing safe, recyclable, and energy-reducing processes.

United States

Giti Tire is continually ramping up production in its full and high-tech new tire production facility in South Carolina, USA. The factory uses top technology and local resources to build passenger car tires for the North American market, including new Giti brand fitments with Volkswagen OEM. A fully-integrated R&D center and office support the facility, which will continue to create jobs, opportunities, and high-quality products for the community and beyond.

Indonesia

Giti Tire's production center in Indonesia, together with partner PT Gajah Tunggal, utilizes decades of production experience to produce efficient and highquality passenger car, truck and bus, and motorcycle tires. To support the facility, a full R&D center and proving ground compliment the production center to allow for all stages of the tire-making and selling process, throughout the ASEAN region as well as other markets worldwide.
